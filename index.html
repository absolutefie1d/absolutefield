Week 1 ‚Äî Foundation

  • Voice leading ‚Äî move each note shortest distance
  • Keep hand in one area of keyboard

  • D, G, A, Bm

10 min inversions ¬∑ 10 min transitions ¬∑ 20 min songs

Week 3 ‚Äî Adding Color

Ongoing Growth Lifetime habits

Weekly Habits

Consistency over intensity

  • Learn 1 new song per week
  • Practice scales in one new key
  • Play along with worship recordings
  • Record yourself and listen back

Monthly Challenges

Push your boundaries

  • Master one complex song
  • Learn a song by ear (no charts)
  • Practice with a metronome at new tempos
  • Try improvising over a progression

Ear Training

Develop your musical ear

  • Identify chord progressions by sound
  • Pick out melodies from recordings
  • Recognize common worship patterns
  • Sing while you play (builds connection)

Music Theory

Understand the "why"

  • Learn why I-IV-V-I works
  • Understand relative minor keys
  • Study chord substitutions
  • Explore modes (start with Mixolydian)
